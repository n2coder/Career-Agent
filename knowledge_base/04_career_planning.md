# Career Planning by Experience Level (India IT) - Updated: February 12, 2026

## 0-2 Years (Entry Stage)
Primary goal: become interview-convertible quickly.

Execution plan:
- Pick one primary role (example: backend) and one backup role.
- Build 3 targeted projects with business-like scope.
- Practice role-specific interview rounds weekly.
- Apply in focused batches with referral-first strategy.

Common mistakes:
- learning too many stacks without finishing projects
- generic resume with no outcomes
- weak fundamentals in SQL/problem solving

## 2-5 Years (Acceleration Stage)
Primary goal: move from executor to owner.

Execution plan:
- Own one service/module end-to-end.
- Add reliability and performance narrative to resume.
- Build system design and debugging depth.
- Switch only when role scope + comp both improve.

Comp strategy:
- evaluate fixed pay vs variable pay clearly
- compare role quality and manager quality, not CTC only

## 5-9 Years (Leverage Stage)
Primary goal: become a high-trust specialist or lead engineer.

Execution plan:
- deepen one technical domain plus one adjacent domain
- mentor juniors and standardize delivery quality
- present architecture decisions with tradeoffs and outcomes

## 9+ Years (Impact Stage)
Primary goal: maximize long-term career compounding.

Execution plan:
- choose IC path vs management path consciously
- optimize for scope, influence, and team quality
- target organizations with strong engineering culture and clear ownership

## 30-60-90 Day Job Search Playbook
## First 30 days
- resume rewrite by role
- project/portfolio cleanup
- interview gap baseline

## Days 31-60
- active pipeline: targeted applications + referrals
- mock rounds and gap closure
- build one additional role-aligned artifact

## Days 61-90
- parallel final-round opportunities
- negotiate using market data + competing loops
- close based on growth path and role quality

## Interview Preparation Grid
- DSA/problem solving: 3 sessions weekly
- system design: 2 sessions weekly (for mid/senior)
- domain rounds: 2 sessions weekly
- behavioral storytelling: 1 session weekly

## Offer Evaluation Checklist
- fixed pay, variable structure, joining bonus, vesting terms
- role scope in first 6 months
- manager and team quality
- tech stack and learning runway
- location, commute, and cost-of-living fit
